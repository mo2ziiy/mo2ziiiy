A bit about me

I believe the best engineers understand both how systems are built — and how they can be broken.

I'm Mohamed Saleh, a 20-year-old developer and cybersecurity student at Horus University, Egypt. I work across two complementary fields: Cybersecurity, with a focus on SOC operations and DFIR, and Frontend Development.

On the development side, I build modern, responsive web applications using React and TypeScript. I use Framer Motion to create smooth, interactive experiences, with a strong focus on clean interfaces, usability, and thoughtful user experience.

On the cybersecurity side, I focus on Security Operations Center practices, including threat detection, log analysis, SIEM workflows, incident response, digital forensics, and DFIR.

This combination of development and security gives me a broader perspective when building, analyzing, and securing digital systems.
